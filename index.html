Violet M. Kimble is a neuroscientist and STEM advocate. She is an alumna of Drew University, where she earned her BS in neuroscience with a minor in statistics.

Currently, Violet is pursuing a Ph.D. in neuroscience at Yale University. Her research interest is applying bioinformatics tools to understand complex brain disorders and diseases.

Outside of academia, Violet works to amplify the voice of underrepresented groups of scientists. She serves as an Outreach Fellow for Yale BBS Diversity and Inclusion Collective (YBDIC) and the Outreach Chair for Yale Society for Advancement of Chicanos/Hispanics and Native Americans in Science (YSACNAS).
